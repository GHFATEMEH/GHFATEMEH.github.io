Research Experience

Undergraduate Research Assistant

Human and Robot Interaction Lab (TaarLab)

During this research, I worked on an automated deep learning‑based hand rub assessment system with real‑time feedback designed in the Human and Robot Interaction Lab. The system evaluates compliance with the 8‑step guideline using a DNN architecture trained on a dataset following the hand rubbing guideline provided by World Health Organization. I design a model using a 3d CNN network to distinguish between real and fake hand rubbing poses. Also, I use a face detection algorithm for recognizing users. In addition, a simple web application wrote to get a log from the system. I worked with an Arduino board, ultrasonic sensor, and jetson NVIDIA Jetson AGX Xavier during this research.

Under the supervision of Dr. Mehdi Tale Masouleh and Dr. Ahmad Kalhor
